Meaning of Each Colour

ColourMeaning
RedIt is drawn from the mountains and embodies the principles of impulse and chaos. The mana symbol for red is represented by a fireball. On the color pie, it is allied with black and green, and is enemies with white and blue. Red seeks freedom through action
BlueIt is drawn from islands and embodies the concepts of logic and technology and seeks perfection through knowledge. The mana symbol for blue is a drop of water. On the color pie, it is allied with white and black and is the enemy of red and green.
GreenIt is drawn from the power of forests and embodies the principles of instinct and interdependence. The mana symbol for green is represented by a tree. On the Color Pie, it is the ally of white and red, and the enemy of blue and black. Green seeks acceptance through growth.
WhiteIt is drawn from the plains and embodies the principles of morality and order. The mana symbol for white is represented by a sun. On the color pie, it is allied with green and blue, and is enemies with black and red. White seeks peace through structure.
BlackIt is drawn from the power of swamps and embodies the principles of parasitism and amorality (though not necessarily immorality). The mana symbol for black is represented by a skull. On the color pie, it is the ally of blue and red, and the enemy of white and green. Black seeks power through ruthlessness or opportunity
