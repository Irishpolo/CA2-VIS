Meaning of Each Colour

ColourMeaning
RedThe color of fire. Red has the ability to burn through your opponent's Life quickly, and mercilessly. Red is all about impulse and passion. Act first, think later.
BlueThe power of control. Blue is all about not letting your opponent make his/her moves, and ensuring your own. It also boasts some of the best draw power in the game.
GreenThe power of nature and growth, green focuses on sheer brute force, and power-ups, with the drawback that some of these cards have high converted mana cost.
WhiteFocuses on healing and restoration, with the ability to utilize many smaller creatures quickly.
BlackThe color of death and decay. Black is about one thing, and one thing only: Winning. Even if it means having to hurt yourself in order to hurt your enemy more, or to hurt yourself to get more.
